# A Painter's Nightmare

A fast paced autoshooter where your spells are created by painting glyphs.

During the day, you paint masterpieces. At night, your nightmares come alive.

Survive ten minutes against increasingly difficult waves of enemies by combining colors, drawing glyphs, and unlocking powerful upgrades.

This game was developed as the final project for the **Workshop on Video Game Design and Development (CC5408)** at the University of Chile by **BitCrawler Studios**.

---

## Gameplay

- Mouse based glyph drawing mechanics
- Color combination spell system
- Progressive enemy waves
- Multiple enemy types
- Boss encounters
- Character upgrades
- Interactive tutorial
- Dynamic lighting and particle effects

---

## Controls

| Action | Control |
|---------|----------|
| Move | WASD |
| Aim | Mouse |
| Draw glyphs | Mouse |
| Cast skills | Space Bar |

---

## Features

### Drawing Based Combat

Instead of selecting abilities from a hotbar, players paint glyphs with the mouse to unleash attacks.

### Color Mixing

Combine colors to unlock stronger abilities.

Examples:

- Red
- Blue
- Purple
- Green
- Orange

Different enemies are vulnerable to different color combinations.

### Enemy Variety

Fight against:

- Flying demons
- Satyrs
- Elemental slimes
- Necromancer boss

Each enemy introduces unique mechanics requiring different strategies.

### Character Progression

Every minute, choose one of three randomly generated upgrades to customize your build.
